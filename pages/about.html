About

Dr. Ripan Ranjan Biswas is a Library Information Assistant at the Learning Resource Center, Indian Institute of Technology Indore, since 2019.

He served as a Library Assistant at the School of Planning and Architecture, Bhopal from 2014 to 2019.

He worked as a Junior Research Fellow (JRF) in Library Science at ADRDE Agra, DRDO, from 2012 to 2014.
